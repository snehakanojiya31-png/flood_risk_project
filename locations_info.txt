Chennai port trust: coastal area near the harbor, historically prone to flooding during heavy monsoon rains and high tide combinations.
Chennai nungambakkam: central residential and commercial area, moderate drainage, occasional waterlogging on main roads during heavy rain.
Sholinganallur: low-lying IT corridor area near wetlands, known for severe flooding during heavy monsoon, especially in 2015.
Alandur: residential area near Adyar river basin, flood-prone during high rainfall and river overflow.
